## Technical Deep Dive
A turbine engine works by using a turbine to drive a compressor, which compresses air that is then mixed with fuel and ignited, producing a high-temperature and high-pressure gas that expands through the turbine, generating power. In a vehicle, this power is transmitted to the wheels through a gearbox and drivetrain. Common failure points in turbine-powered vehicles include the turbine itself, the compressor, and the gearbox. The 'Krispy Kritter' Mustang drag racer, for example, uses a turbine engine wrapped in a Mustang body, with a few additional parts to make it roll. This unique setup requires careful consideration of the vehicle's systems and components to ensure optimal performance and reliability.

## Step-by-Step Guide
### Turbine Engine Overhaul
#### Step 1: Disassemble the Turbine Engine
To begin the overhaul process, carefully disassemble the turbine engine, taking note of the various components and their relationships. This will involve removing the compressor, turbine, and gearbox, as well as any additional components such as fuel injectors and ignition systems.
#### Step 2: Inspect and Clean the Compressor
Next, inspect the compressor for any signs of wear or damage, and clean it thoroughly using a compressor cleaning kit. This will help ensure optimal performance and prevent any issues with the turbine engine.
#### Step 3: Replace the Turbine
If necessary, replace the turbine with a new or refurbished unit, ensuring proper installation and alignment. This will involve careful measurement and adjustment to ensure the turbine is properly seated and secured.
### Gearbox Repair
#### Step 1: Disassemble the Gearbox
To repair the gearbox, begin by disassembling it, taking note of the various components and their relationships. This will involve removing the gear sets, bearings, and seals, as well as any additional components such as the gearbox housing.
#### Step 2: Inspect and Replace Worn Components
Next, inspect the gearbox components for any signs of wear or damage, and replace them as necessary. This will involve careful measurement and adjustment to ensure the gearbox is properly assembled and functioning correctly.
#### Step 3: Reassemble the Gearbox
Finally, reassemble the gearbox, ensuring proper installation and alignment of all components. This will involve careful measurement and adjustment to ensure the gearbox is properly seated and secured.
