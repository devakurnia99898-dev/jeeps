## Step-by-Step Guide
### Introduction to Maintenance
Regular maintenance is key to extending the life of the Ora Ballet Cat's new powertrain. This includes routine checks and replacements of essential components.

#### Checking Engine Oil
1. Locate the oil dipstick under the hood.
2. Pull out the dipstick and wipe it clean with a lint-free cloth.
3. Insert the dipstick back into its tube and pull it out again to get an accurate reading.
4. Check the oil level against the minimum and maximum marks on the dipstick.

#### Replacing the Air Filter
1. Locate the air filter housing, typically in the engine compartment.
2. Release the clips or screws holding the housing in place.
3. Pull out the old air filter and discard it.
4. Install a new air filter, ensuring it is properly seated and the housing is securely closed.

### Advanced Maintenance
For more advanced tasks, such as transmission service or spark plug replacement, refer to the vehicle's manual or consult with a professional mechanic.

#### Spark Plug Replacement
##### Preparation
- Ensure the vehicle is parked on a level surface and apply the parking brake.
- Allow the engine to cool down to avoid burns.

##### Removal
1. Locate the spark plugs, typically on the sides of the engine.
2. Use a spark plug socket to remove the spark plugs.
3. Pull out the spark plugs and set them aside.

##### Installation
1. Take the new spark plugs and apply a thin layer of spark plug thread lubricant.
2. Insert the new spark plugs into their respective holes.
3. Tighten the spark plugs in a star pattern to the specified torque (typically around 10-15 ft-lbs).
