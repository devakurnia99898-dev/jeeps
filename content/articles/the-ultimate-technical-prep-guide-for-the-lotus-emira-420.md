## Step-by-Step Guide
### Step 1: Prepare the Vehicle
Before starting any work on your Lotus Emira 420 Sport, make sure it is parked on a level surface and apply the parking brake. Engage the transmission in first gear and turn off the engine.

#### Step 1.1: Locate the Oil Drain Plug
The oil drain plug is located at the bottom of the engine, near the front of the vehicle. Use a socket wrench to remove the plug and allow the oil to drain into a pan.

#### Step 1.2: Replace the Oil Filter
The oil filter is located on top of the engine, near the oil filler cap. Use a filter wrench to remove the old filter and replace it with a new one.

### Step 2: Inspect the Suspension
The Lotus Emira 420 Sport's suspension is a critical component of its performance. Inspect the suspension for any signs of wear or damage, including loose or worn-out bushings, ball joints, or control arms.

#### Step 2.1: Check the Bushings
The bushings are located at the ends of the control arms and are responsible for absorbing shocks and vibrations. Use a bushing compressor to remove the old bushings and replace them with new ones.

#### Step 2.2: Inspect the Ball Joints
The ball joints are located at the ends of the control arms and are responsible for connecting the suspension to the steering system. Use a ball joint separator to remove the old ball joints and replace them with new ones.

### Step 3: Check the Brakes
The Lotus Emira 420 Sport's brakes are a critical component of its safety. Inspect the brakes for any signs of wear or damage, including worn-out brake pads, rotors, or calipers.

#### Step 3.1: Check the Brake Pads
The brake pads are located inside the brake calipers and are responsible for applying pressure to the rotors. Use a brake pad wear gauge to check the thickness of the brake pads and replace them if necessary.

#### Step 3.2: Inspect the Rotors
The rotors are located on the wheels and are responsible for absorbing the heat generated by the brakes. Use a rotor resurfacing tool to resurface the rotors if they are worn out.
