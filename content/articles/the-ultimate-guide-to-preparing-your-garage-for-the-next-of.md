## Technical Deep Dive

The electric and hybrid powertrains, for example, will require specialized tools and knowledge of high-voltage systems. The advanced safety features, such as adaptive cruise control and lane departure warning, will require an understanding of complex software and sensor systems. The sophisticated infotainment systems will require knowledge of advanced networking and connectivity protocols.

Common failure points in these systems include the high-voltage battery, electric motor, and power electronics. The advanced safety features can be prone to issues with sensor calibration and software updates. The infotainment systems can be susceptible to problems with connectivity and compatibility.
