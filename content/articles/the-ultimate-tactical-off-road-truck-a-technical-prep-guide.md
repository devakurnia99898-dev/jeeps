## Step-by-Step Guide
### Step 1: Pre-Delivery Inspection
Before taking delivery of the Rezvani Fortress, it's crucial to conduct a thorough inspection to identify any potential issues. This includes checking the suspension, engine, and electrical systems for any signs of damage or wear.

#### Step 1.1: Visual Inspection
Conduct a visual inspection of the vehicle, looking for any signs of damage or wear on the suspension components, such as the shocks and control arms.

#### Step 1.2: System Checks
Perform system checks on the engine, transmission, and electrical systems to ensure they are functioning correctly.

### Step 2: Suspension Upgrades
The Rezvani Fortress is likely to come with a robust suspension system, but upgrading it can further improve its off-road capabilities. This includes installing heavier-duty shocks and control arms, as well as lifting the vehicle to increase ground clearance.

#### Step 2.1: Shock Upgrade
Install heavier-duty shocks, such as coilovers or bypass shocks, to improve the vehicle's suspension travel and stability.

#### Step 2.2: Control Arm Upgrade
Install heavier-duty control arms to improve the vehicle's suspension stability and articulation.

#### Step 2.3: Lift Kit Installation
Install a lift kit to increase the vehicle's ground clearance and improve its approach and departure angles.

### Step 3: Engine Modifications

#### Step 3.2: Exhaust System Installation
