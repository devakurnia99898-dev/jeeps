### Step 1: Suspension Inspection
The suspension system is a critical component of the new Australian ute, and regular inspection is essential to ensure optimal performance. To inspect the suspension, follow these steps:
#### Step 1.1: Raise the vehicle
Use a hydraulic lift or jack to raise the vehicle, ensuring it is securely supported by jack stands.
#### Step 1.2: Remove the wheels
Remove the wheels to access the suspension components.
#### Step 1.3: Inspect the coil springs
Check the coil springs for signs of wear or damage, such as cracks or corrosion.
#### Step 1.4: Inspect the shock absorbers
Check the shock absorbers for signs of wear or damage, such as leaks or corrosion.
