## Technical Deep Dive
The C4 Corvette was produced from 1984 to 1996 and was the fourth generation of the Corvette series. The 1995 C4 Corvette Indy Pace Car was a special edition model, produced to commemorate the Corvette's role as the pace car for the 1995 Indianapolis 500. It featured a unique exterior color scheme, with a black and yellow paint job, and a number of interior upgrades, including a special edition steering wheel and seat trim.

## Step-by-Step Guide
### Step 1: Inspection and Planning
#### Step 1.1: Visual Inspection
Start by visually inspecting the car, looking for any signs of damage or wear and tear. Check the exterior paint and bodywork, as well as the interior trim and upholstery. Make a list of any areas that need attention.

#### Step 1.2: Mechanical Inspection
Next, inspect the car's mechanical systems, including the engine, transmission, and brakes. Check the oil and fluid levels, and look for any signs of leaks or damage. Make a list of any areas that need attention.

#### Step 1.3: Planning and Budgeting
Based on your inspection, create a plan and budget for the restoration. Decide which areas to prioritize, and allocate your resources accordingly. Make sure to include a contingency fund for any unexpected expenses.

### Step 2: Exterior Restoration
#### Step 2.1: Paint and Bodywork
Start by repairing any damage to the exterior paint and bodywork. This may involve sanding and repainting the car, as well as replacing any damaged trim or moldings.

#### Step 2.2: Headlight System Repair
Next, repair the pop-up headlight system. This may involve replacing the headlight motors, as well as the headlight lenses and trim.

#### Step 2.3: Rear Spoiler Repair
Finally, repair the rear spoiler. This may involve replacing the spoiler itself, as well as any damaged trim or moldings.

### Step 3: Interior Restoration
#### Step 3.1: Upholstery and Trim
Start by repairing or replacing the interior upholstery and trim. This may involve reupholstering the seats, as well as replacing the dashboard and door trim.

#### Step 3.2: Carpet and Floor Mats
Next, replace the carpet and floor mats. This will help to improve the overall appearance and comfort of the car.

#### Step 3.3: Electronics and Accessories
Finally, repair or replace any damaged electronics or accessories, such as the radio or air conditioning system.
