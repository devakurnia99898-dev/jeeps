# Step-by-Step Guide
In this section, we'll provide a detailed, step-by-step guide to performing regular maintenance on your Jeep.

### Step 1: Oil Change
#### Step 1.1: Gather materials and prepare the vehicle
Before starting the oil change, gather all the necessary materials, including the new oil filter, oil, and drain pan. Ensure the vehicle is parked on a level surface and apply the parking brake.

#### Step 1.2: Warm up the engine and locate the oil drain plug
Start the engine and let it run for a few minutes to warm up the oil. Locate the oil drain plug, which is usually located at the bottom of the engine.

#### Step 1.3: Drain the oil and replace the filter
Use a socket wrench to remove the oil drain plug and allow the oil to drain into the pan. Once the oil has stopped dripping, replace the plug and tighten it to the specified torque (usually around 30 ft-lbs).

### Step 2: Filter Replacement
#### Step 2.1: Locate the air filter and remove the housing
The air filter is usually located in the engine compartment, and is held in place by a housing or cover. Remove the housing to access the filter.

#### Step 2.2: Remove the old filter and install the new one
Pull out the old filter and discard it. Install the new filter, ensuring it is properly seated and secured.

#### Step 2.3: Replace the housing and check the filter
Replace the housing and ensure it is properly secured. Check the filter to ensure it is properly installed and functioning.

### Step 3: Suspension Inspection
#### Step 3.1: Raise the vehicle and support it
Raise the vehicle using a lift or jack, and support it using jack stands. Ensure the vehicle is properly secured before starting work.

#### Step 3.2: Inspect the suspension components
Inspect the suspension components, including the bushings, joints, and shocks. Look for signs of wear or damage, and replace any components that are damaged or worn out.

#### Step 3.3: Lubricate the suspension components
Lubricate the suspension components, including the bushings and joints. Use a silicone-based lubricant to ensure the components are properly protected.
