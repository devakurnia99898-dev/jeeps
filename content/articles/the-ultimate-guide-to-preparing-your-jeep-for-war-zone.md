## Technical Deep Dive
When it comes to preparing your Jeep for war zone expeditions, it's essential to understand the system works. The Jeep's engine, suspension, and electrical systems must be modified to withstand the harsh conditions of a war zone. Common failure points include:
* The plastic impeller in the water pump, which can shatter under extreme temperatures
* The suspension system, which can be damaged by rough terrain
* The electrical system, which can be compromised by electromagnetic pulses (EMPs)

To mitigate these risks, we recommend:
* Upgrading to a heavy-duty water pump with a metal impeller
* Installing a suspension lift kit with heavy-duty shocks and springs
* Installing an EMP-resistant electrical system with a backup battery and alternator
