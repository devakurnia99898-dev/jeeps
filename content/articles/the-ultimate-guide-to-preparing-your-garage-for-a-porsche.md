## Step-by-Step Guide
### Prepare the Garage
#### Install adequate lighting
A well-lit garage is essential for working on high-performance vehicles. Install LED lighting to provide ample illumination and reduce eye strain.

#### Ensure proper ventilation
Proper ventilation is critical when working with high-performance vehicles. Install a ventilation system that can remove fumes and particles from the air.

#### Organize tools and equipment
Organize your tools and equipment to ensure easy access and reduce clutter. Invest in a tool chest and label each tool to avoid confusion.

### Prepare the Vehicle
#### Research the vehicle's specifications
Research the Porsche hypercar's specifications, including the engine, transmission, and suspension system. Understand the unique requirements of the vehicle and prepare your garage accordingly.

#### Familiarize yourself with the vehicle's systems
Familiarize yourself with the vehicle's systems, including the high-voltage electrical system, advanced computer system, and specialized engine components. Understand how each system works and how to maintain them.

#### Develop a maintenance schedule
Develop a maintenance schedule to ensure the vehicle receives regular maintenance. Include routine checks on the engine, transmission, and suspension system, as well as regular software updates for the advanced computer system.
