## Technical Deep Dive
The Tesla Model S and X feature advanced electric powertrains, with the Model S boasting a 100D or P100D variant, and the Model X offering a 100D or P100D variant as well. The electric motor is powered by a high-performance battery pack, which is managed by Tesla's sophisticated battery management system. Common failure points in these systems include the battery pack's thermal management system, the electric motor's bearings, and the high-voltage electrical system's connectors and wiring.

## Step-by-Step Guide
### Step 1: Tire Rotation and Inspection
Tire rotation is an essential maintenance task that can help extend the life of your tires and improve your vehicle's overall performance. To rotate your tires, follow these steps:
#### Step 1.1: Gather necessary tools and equipment
* 10mm socket
* Tire pressure gauge
* Wheel chocks
#### Step 1.2: Loosen the lug nuts
* Before raising the vehicle, loosen the lug nuts on the wheels using a 10mm socket
* Turn the lug nuts counterclockwise until they are loose, but do not remove them yet
#### Step 1.3: Raise the vehicle
* Raise the vehicle using a hydraulic lift or jack stands
* Ensure the vehicle is securely supported and on level ground
#### Step 1.4: Remove the lug nuts and wheels
* Completely remove the lug nuts and wheels
* Set the wheels aside and inspect the brake rotors and pads for wear

### Step 2: Brake Pad Replacement
Brake pad replacement is a critical maintenance task that can help ensure your safety on the road. To replace your brake pads, follow these steps:
#### Step 2.1: Gather necessary tools and equipment
* 10mm socket
* Torque wrench
* Brake pad spreader
* New brake pads
#### Step 2.2: Remove the caliper
* Remove the caliper using a 10mm socket
* Set the caliper aside and inspect the brake rotor for wear
#### Step 2.3: Remove the old brake pads
* Remove the old brake pads from the caliper
* Inspect the brake pads for wear and damage
#### Step 2.4: Install the new brake pads
* Install the new brake pads into the caliper
* Ensure the brake pads are properly seated and aligned

### Step 3: Battery Software Update
Battery software updates can help improve the performance and efficiency of your vehicle's battery pack. To update your battery software, follow these steps:
#### Step 3.1: Connect to the vehicle's computer system
* Connect to the vehicle's computer system using a multimeter and high-voltage electrical system tester
* Ensure the vehicle is in a well-ventilated area and the high-voltage electrical system is disabled
#### Step 3.2: Download the software update
* Download the software update from Tesla's website
* Ensure the update is compatible with your vehicle's software version
#### Step 3.3: Install the software update
* Install the software update using the multimeter and high-voltage electrical system tester
* Ensure the update is properly installed and the vehicle's computer system is functioning correctly

## Frequently Asked Questions
Here are some frequently asked questions about maintaining your luxury electric vehicle:
1. Q: How often should I rotate my tires?
A: It's recommended to rotate your tires every 5,000 to 7,000 miles to ensure even wear and extend the life of your tires.
2. Q: What are the symptoms of worn-out brake pads?
A: Symptoms of worn-out brake pads include squealing or grinding noises, vibration, and decreased brake performance.
3. Q: Can I update my battery software myself?
A: Yes, you can update your battery software yourself using a multimeter and high-voltage electrical system tester. However, it's recommended to consult the manufacturer's instructions and guidelines to ensure the update is properly installed.
4. Q: How often should I inspect my brake rotors?
A: It's recommended to inspect your brake rotors every 10,000 to 15,000 miles to ensure they are not worn out or damaged.
5. Q: What are the benefits of using high-performance tires?
A: High-performance tires can improve your vehicle's handling, braking, and acceleration. They can also provide better traction and control on various road surfaces.
