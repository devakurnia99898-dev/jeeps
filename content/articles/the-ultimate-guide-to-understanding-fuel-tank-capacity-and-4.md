## Technical Deep Dive
The fuel tank capacity of a Jeep varies depending on the model and year. For example, the Jeep Wrangler has a fuel tank capacity of 18.5 gallons, while the Jeep Grand Cherokee has a capacity of 24.6 gallons. The 4-gallon minimum rule is crucial when using a blender pump, as it ensures that the fuel pump can accurately measure the fuel level and prevent overfilling or underfilling.
