## Step-by-Step Guide
### Step 1: Planning and Preparation
#### Step 1.1: Research and Choose a Design
Research different interior design options, including the Carhartt package, and choose a style that suits your preferences. Consider factors like durability, comfort, and aesthetics.
#### Step 1.2: Gather Materials and Tools
Gather all the necessary materials and tools, including the Carhartt-inspired interior kit, upholstery fabric, and interior trim removal tools.

### Step 2: Removing the Old Interior
#### Step 2.1: Disconnect the Battery
Disconnect the battery to avoid any short circuits or electrical shocks.
#### Step 2.2: Remove the Dashboard Trim
Remove the dashboard trim and any other components that may be obstructing access to the interior.
#### Step 2.3: Remove the Old Seats and Upholstery
Remove the old seats and upholstery, taking care not to damage any underlying components.

### Step 3: Installing the New Interior
#### Step 3.1: Install the New Seats and Upholstery
Install the new seats and upholstery, ensuring a snug and secure fit.
#### Step 3.2: Install the Carhartt-Inspired Interior Kit
Install the Carhartt-inspired interior kit, following the manufacturer's instructions.
#### Step 3.3: Reinstall the Dashboard Trim
Reinstall the dashboard trim and any other components that were removed.

### Step 4: Final Touches
#### Step 4.1: Add Carhartt Graphics
Add Carhartt graphics to the interior, following the manufacturer's instructions.
#### Step 4.2: Install the Custom Grille
Install the custom grille, ensuring a secure and even fit.
