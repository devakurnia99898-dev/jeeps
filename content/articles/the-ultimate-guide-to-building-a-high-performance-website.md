## Technical Deep Dive
A high-performance website is built on a solid foundation of technical expertise. This includes a fast and reliable web hosting service, a well-designed and user-friendly interface, and a robust content management system. When it comes to building a website for your Jeep business, it's essential to consider the following technical aspects:

* **Content Management System (CMS)**: A CMS is the backbone of your website, allowing you to create, edit, and manage your content. Popular CMS options include WordPress, Joomla, and Drupal.
* **Web Hosting**: Your web hosting service provides the infrastructure for your website to exist on the internet. Look for a reliable and fast web hosting service that can handle high traffic and provides excellent customer support.
* **Search Engine Optimization (SEO)**: SEO is the process of optimizing your website to rank higher in search engine results pages (SERPs). This includes keyword research, on-page optimization, and link building.

Common failure points to watch out for include slow page loading times, poor mobile responsiveness, and lack of SEO optimization.

## Pro Tips & Common Mistakes
* **Don't** use too many plugins, as this can slow down your website.
* **Do** use a website builder to create a custom design.
* **Don't** forget to optimize your images and compress files to reduce page loading times.
* **Do** use a security plugin to protect your website from malware and hacking attempts.
