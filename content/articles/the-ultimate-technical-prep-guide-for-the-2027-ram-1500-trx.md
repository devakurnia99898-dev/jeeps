## Step-by-Step Guide
### Understanding the Warranty and Ownership Terms
The 2027 Ram 1500 TRX SRT comes with a comprehensive warranty package that includes a 3-year/36,000-mile basic warranty, a 5-year/60,000-mile powertrain warranty, and a 5-year/100,000-mile diesel engine warranty. However, the warranty is subject to certain terms and conditions, including a financial penalty for early sale or transfer.

#### Reviewing the Warranty Documents
Review the warranty documents carefully to understand the terms and conditions, including the financial penalty for early sale or transfer.

#### Understanding the Financial Penalty
The financial penalty for early sale or transfer is a significant amount, and owners should carefully consider the implications before making a decision.

### Maintaining the Vehicle
Regular maintenance is essential to ensure the longevity and performance of the vehicle.

#### Oil Change
Change the oil every 5,000 miles or as recommended by the manufacturer. Use a high-quality oil filter and make sure to dispose of the used oil and filter responsibly.

#### Tire Rotation
Rotate the tires every 6,000 miles or as recommended by the manufacturer. Use a tire rotation pattern to ensure even wear and extend the life of the tires.

#### Suspension Inspection
Inspect the suspension system every 12,000 miles or as recommended by the manufacturer. Check for signs of wear and damage, and replace any worn or damaged components.

#### Supercharger Belt Replacement
Replace the supercharger belt every 50,000 miles or as recommended by the manufacturer. Use a high-quality belt and follow the manufacturer's instructions for replacement.
