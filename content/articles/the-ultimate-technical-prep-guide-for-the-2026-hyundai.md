## Step-by-Step Guide
### Step 1: Prepare the Vehicle
#### Step 1.1: Disconnect the Battery
Disconnect the negative terminal of the battery to prevent any accidental starts or electrical shocks.

#### Step 1.2: Remove the Trim and Dashboard Components
Remove the trim and dashboard components to access the infotainment system and wiring.

### Step 2: Access the Infotainment System
#### Step 2.1: Locate the Infotainment Unit
Locate the infotainment unit and identify the wiring and connections.

#### Step 2.2: Disconnect the Wiring and Connections
Disconnect the wiring and connections to the infotainment unit, taking care not to damage any of the components.

### Step 3: Upgrade the Infotainment System
#### Step 3.1: Install the New Display Screen
Install the new display screen, ensuring it is properly secured and connected to the infotainment unit.

#### Step 3.2: Install the New Processor and Memory
Install the new processor and memory, ensuring they are properly connected and configured.

### Step 4: Reassemble the Vehicle
#### Step 4.1: Reconnect the Wiring and Connections
Reconnect the wiring and connections to the infotainment unit, ensuring they are secure and properly connected.

#### Step 4.2: Reinstall the Trim and Dashboard Components
Reinstall the trim and dashboard components, ensuring they are properly secured and aligned.
