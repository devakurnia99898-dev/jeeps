## **Technical Deep Dive**
The Toyota Supra's engine has undergone significant changes over the years, with the most notable being the switch from a naturally aspirated engine to a turbocharged one. The current 3.0-liter inline-6 engine produces 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. However, to achieve maximum performance, it's essential to understand how the engine works and common failure points. The engine's system consists of a turbocharger, intercooler, and a dual-scroll turbocharger. Common failure points include the turbocharger's bearings, the intercooler's core, and the engine's oil pump.

## **Step-by-Step Guide**
Refining the Supra's engine requires a thorough understanding of the engine's system and components. Below is a step-by-step guide on how to refine the engine:
### **Step 1: Prepare the Vehicle**
#### **Step 1.1: Jack Up the Vehicle**
Raise the vehicle using a hydraulic jack, ensuring it's securely supported by jack stands. Remove the wheels and set them aside.
#### **Step 1.2: Remove the Engine Cover**
Remove the engine cover to access the engine's components. Use a 10mm socket to remove the bolts holding the cover in place.
### **Step 2: Inspect the Engine**
#### **Step 2.1: Check the Oil Level**
Check the engine's oil level to ensure it's at the recommended level. Use a dipstick to check the oil level.
#### **Step 2.2: Inspect the Air Filter**
Inspect the air filter to ensure it's clean and free of debris. Replace the air filter if necessary.
### **Step 3: Refine the Engine**
#### **Step 3.1: Upgrade the Turbocharger**
Upgrade the turbocharger to a high-performance unit. Use a 12mm socket to remove the bolts holding the turbocharger in place.
#### **Step 3.2: Install a Performance Intercooler**
Install a performance intercooler to improve the engine's cooling system. Use a torque wrench to secure the intercooler in place, tightening the bolts to 30 ft-lbs.
### **Step 4: Tune the Engine**
#### **Step 4.1: Use a Tuning Software**
Use a tuning software to adjust the engine's settings and optimize performance. Ensure the software is compatible with the Supra's engine.
#### **Step 4.2: Monitor the Engine's Performance**
Monitor the engine's performance using a dyno or a performance meter. Adjust the settings as necessary to achieve optimal performance.
