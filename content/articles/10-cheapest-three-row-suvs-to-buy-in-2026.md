### Early Years: CJ Series
The CJ series, which stands for Civilian Jeep, was the first mass-produced off-road vehicle. It was based on the Willys MB, a military vehicle used during World War II. The CJ series was produced from 1941 to 1986 and was known for its ruggedness and reliability.

### Evolution of the Wrangler: YJ, TJ, and JK
The YJ, TJ, and JK models were the successors to the CJ series. The YJ, produced from 1987 to 1995, was the first Wrangler model to feature a more modern design and improved off-road capabilities. The TJ, produced from 1997 to 2006, further improved upon the YJ's design and added more features. The JK, produced from 2007 to 2018, was a major overhaul of the Wrangler design and featured a more modern and refined interior.
