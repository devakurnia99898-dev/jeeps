## Step-by-Step Guide
### Install High-Power Charging Station
The first step in preparing your garage for the Kia EV sports sedan is to install a high-power charging station. This will require a significant upgrade to your electrical infrastructure, including the installation of a new 240-volt circuit and a high-power charging cable.

#### Determine the Required Charging Power
To determine the required charging power, you'll need to consult the specifications of the Kia EV sports sedan. The vehicle's charging system will likely require a minimum of 11.5 kW of power to recharge the batteries quickly.

#### Install the Charging Station
Once you've determined the required charging power, you can install the high-power charging station. This will involve mounting the charging unit to the wall, connecting the electrical connections, and installing the charging cable.

#### Test the Charging Station
After installation, it's essential to test the charging station to ensure that it's functioning correctly. Use a multimeter to verify that the charging station is outputting the correct voltage and current.

### Upgrade Garage Ventilation System
The next step is to upgrade your garage ventilation system to ensure that the Kia EV sports sedan has a well-ventilated environment to operate in. This will involve installing a new ventilation system, including a high-performance fan and ductwork.

#### Determine the Required Ventilation Rate
To determine the required ventilation rate, you'll need to consult the specifications of the Kia EV sports sedan. The vehicle's ventilation system will likely require a minimum of 100 CFM of airflow to maintain a stable temperature.

#### Install the Ventilation System
Once you've determined the required ventilation rate, you can install the new ventilation system. This will involve mounting the fan to the wall, connecting the ductwork, and installing the ventilation grille.

#### Test the Ventilation System
After installation, it's essential to test the ventilation system to ensure that it's functioning correctly. Use a thermal imaging camera to verify that the ventilation system is maintaining a stable temperature and preventing overheating.

### Install Temperature Control System
The final step is to install a temperature control system to maintain a stable temperature in your garage. This will involve installing a new thermostat and temperature control unit.

#### Determine the Required Temperature Range
To determine the required temperature range, you'll need to consult the specifications of the Kia EV sports sedan. The vehicle's temperature control system will likely require a temperature range of 60°F to 80°F (15°C to 27°C) to maintain optimal performance.

#### Install the Temperature Control System
Once you've determined the required temperature range, you can install the new temperature control system. This will involve mounting the thermostat to the wall, connecting the electrical connections, and installing the temperature control unit.

#### Test the Temperature Control System
After installation, it's essential to test the temperature control system to ensure that it's functioning correctly. Use a thermometer to verify that the temperature control system is maintaining the required temperature range.
