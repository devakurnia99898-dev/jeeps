## Step-by-Step Guide
### Introduction to Maintenance
Regular maintenance is essential to keep high-performance vehicles like the Mercedes 500E running smoothly. This includes regular oil changes, spark plug replacement, and timing chain maintenance.

#### Oil Change
1. Locate the oil drain plug underneath the vehicle and remove it using a 10mm socket.
2. Allow the oil to drain into a pan for at least 10 minutes.
3. Replace the oil filter with a new one and fill the engine with the recommended type and amount of oil.

#### Spark Plug Replacement
1. Locate the spark plugs and remove the spark plug wires.
2. Use a spark plug socket to remove the spark plugs.
3. Install new spark plugs and replace the spark plug wires.

#### Timing Chain Replacement
1. Remove the timing chain cover and locate the timing chain.
2. Mark the position of the timing chain and remove it.
3. Install a new timing chain and replace the timing chain cover.

### Advanced Maintenance
Advanced maintenance tasks for high-performance vehicles like the Mercedes 500E include fuel injector cleaning, ignition system maintenance, and suspension alignment.

#### Fuel Injector Cleaning
1. Remove the fuel injectors and clean them using a fuel injector cleaning kit.
2. Install new fuel injector seals and replace the fuel injectors.

#### Ignition System Maintenance
1. Remove the ignition coil and spark plug wires.
2. Clean the ignition coil and spark plug wires using a soft brush and electrical cleaner.
3. Install new spark plug wires and replace the ignition coil.

#### Suspension Alignment
1. Raise the vehicle on a lift and remove the wheels.
2. Use a suspension alignment kit to adjust the suspension.
3. Replace the wheels and lower the vehicle to the ground.

## Pro Tips & Common Mistakes
When maintaining high-performance vehicles like the Mercedes 500E, it is essential to avoid common mistakes that can lead to costly repairs. These include:

* Not following the recommended maintenance schedule
* Using the wrong type of oil or fuel
* Not tightening bolts and fasteners properly

Pro tips for maintaining high-performance vehicles include:

* Using a torque wrench to ensure bolts and fasteners are tightened to the correct specification
* Using a socket set with a range of sizes to avoid damaging bolts and fasteners
* Keeping a maintenance log to track maintenance tasks and ensure they are completed on time
