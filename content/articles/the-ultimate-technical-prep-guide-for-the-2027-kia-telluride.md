## Technical Deep Dive
The 2027 Kia Telluride is expected to feature a 3.8-liter V6 engine, producing 291 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque. The engine will be paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and available all-wheel drive. The suspension system will likely feature a multi-link rear setup and a MacPherson strut front suspension.

Common failure points in the Kia Telluride include the water pump, timing chain, and engine mounts. The water pump is prone to failure due to the plastic impeller, which can crack or break over time. The timing chain can stretch or jump teeth, causing engine misfires and poor performance. Engine mounts can wear out, leading to vibrations and noise.

## Step-by-Step Guide
### Preparation and Inspection
Before starting any work on the 2027 Kia Telluride, it's essential to inspect the vehicle and gather the necessary tools and materials.
#### Checking the Owner's Manual
Familiarize yourself with the owner's manual to understand the recommended maintenance schedule and procedures.
#### Inspecting the Vehicle
Check the vehicle for any signs of damage or wear, including dents, scratches, and tire wear.

### Oil Change
Changing the oil in the 2027 Kia Telluride is a relatively straightforward process.
#### Draining the Oil
Locate the oil drain plug and use a 10mm socket to remove it. Allow the oil to drain into a pan.
#### Replacing the Oil Filter
Use a filter wrench to remove the oil filter. Install a new oil filter and hand tighten it.
#### Refilling the Oil
Pour in the recommended type and amount of oil. Replace the oil cap and start the engine.

### Tire Rotation
Rotating the tires on the 2027 Kia Telluride is essential for even wear and prolonged lifespan.
#### Loosening the Lug Nuts
Before raising the vehicle, loosen the lug nuts on the first wheel using a 12mm socket.
#### Raising the Vehicle
Use a jack to raise the vehicle and remove the wheel.
#### Rotating the Tires
Rotate the tires according to the recommended pattern.

### Brake Pad Replacement
Replacing the brake pads on the 2027 Kia Telluride requires more expertise and specialized tools.
#### Removing the Caliper
Use a 10mm socket to remove the caliper bolts. Slide the caliper off the rotor.
#### Removing the Old Brake Pads
Pull out the old brake pads and discard them.
#### Installing the New Brake Pads
Install the new brake pads and reassemble the caliper.

## Pro Tips & Common Mistakes
* Always refer to the owner's manual for specific instructions and recommendations.
* Use the correct type and amount of oil to avoid engine damage.
* Never overtighten the lug nuts, as this can damage the wheel bearings.
* Use a torque wrench to ensure accurate torque specs.
