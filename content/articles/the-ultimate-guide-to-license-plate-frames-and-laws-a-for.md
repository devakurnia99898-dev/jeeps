## Technical Deep Dive
The system works by requiring license plates to be mounted on the vehicle in a specific manner. The license plate frame is designed to hold the plate in place, while also providing a secure attachment point to the vehicle. Common failure points include:

* Incorrectly mounted license plates
* Obstructed license plate views due to frame design or mounting
* Failure to comply with state regulations

In the case of Jeep owners, it's essential to understand the specific regulations in their state. For example, some states require license plates to be mounted on the rear of the vehicle, while others allow for front and rear mounting. Understanding these regulations can help Jeep owners avoid unnecessary fines or arrests.
