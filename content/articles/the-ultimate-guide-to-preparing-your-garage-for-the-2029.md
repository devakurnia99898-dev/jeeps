## Technical Deep Dive
The 2029 Honda Element Hybrid will likely feature a combination of a gasoline engine and an electric motor. The hybrid system will work together to provide improved fuel efficiency and reduced emissions. Common components of a hybrid system include:
* Electric motor
* Battery pack
* Power control unit (PCU)
* Regenerative braking system
* Hybrid control unit (HCU)
* Engine control unit (ECU)

Common failure points in hybrid vehicles include:
* Battery pack degradation
* Electric motor failure
* Power control unit (PCU) malfunction
* Regenerative braking system failure
