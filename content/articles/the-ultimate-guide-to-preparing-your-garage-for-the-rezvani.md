## Step-by-Step Guide

The first step in preparing your garage for the Rezvani Dune is to ensure that you have a suitable workspace. This includes a level, flat surface, adequate lighting, and sufficient ventilation. ### Inspecting and Maintaining the Rezvani Dune
Regular inspection and maintenance are critical to ensuring the longevity and performance of the Rezvani Dune. This includes regular checks of the engine oil, coolant, and brake fluid, as well as more detailed inspections of the suspension and steering systems.

#### Checking the Engine Oil
To check the engine oil, follow these steps:
1. Locate the oil dipstick under the hood.
2. Pull out the dipstick and wipe it clean with a rag.
3. Insert the dipstick back into the oil reservoir and pull it out again.
4. Check the oil level and color, looking for any signs of contamination or wear.

#### Checking the Coolant
To check the coolant, follow these steps:
1. Locate the coolant reservoir under the hood.
2. Check the coolant level, looking for any signs of leakage or contamination.
3. Check the color and consistency of the coolant, looking for any signs of wear or degradation.

### Advanced Maintenance and Repair
More advanced maintenance and repair tasks, such as replacing the engine or suspension components, require specialized tools and expertise. It's essential to consult the owner's manual or seek the advice of a qualified mechanic before attempting these tasks.

#### Replacing the Engine
To replace the engine, follow these steps:
1. Disconnect the battery and any other electrical connections.
2. Remove the engine mounts and any other supporting components.
3. Carefully lift the engine out of the engine compartment.
4. Install the new engine, following the manufacturer's instructions.

#### Replacing the Suspension
To replace the suspension, follow these steps:
1. Raise the vehicle on a lift or jack stands.
2. Remove the wheels and any other components that may be in the way.
3. Disconnect the suspension links and any other connections.
4. Carefully remove the old suspension components and install the new ones.
