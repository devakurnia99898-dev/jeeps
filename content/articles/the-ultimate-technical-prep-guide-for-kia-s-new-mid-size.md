### Performing Routine Maintenance
#### Oil Change
1. Locate the oil drain plug and position the oil drain pan underneath
2. Remove the oil drain plug and allow the oil to drain completely
3. Replace the oil drain plug and tighten to the specified torque (30 ft-lbs)
4. Fill the engine with the recommended type and amount of oil (5W-30, 6 quarts)

#### Tire Rotation
1. Locate the tire rotation pattern in the owner's manual or on the tire information placard
2. Loosen the lug nuts on the first wheel using a torque wrench (100 ft-lbs)
3. Raise the vehicle using a jack and support it with jack stands
4. Remove the lug nuts and flat washers, and pull the wheel straight off the hub
5. Repeat the process for the remaining wheels, following the recommended tire rotation pattern

### Troubleshooting Common Issues
#### Engine Performance Issues
1. Check the engine oil level and condition
2. Inspect the air filter and replace if necessary
3. Check the spark plugs and replace if necessary
4. Use a scan tool to diagnose any trouble codes or issues with the engine control module

#### Suspension Component Replacement
1. Raise the vehicle using a jack and support it with jack stands
2. Remove the wheel and caliper, and disconnect the shock absorber or coil spring
3. Remove the control arm or ball joint, and inspect for wear or damage
4. Replace the component with a new one, and tighten to the specified torque (50 ft-lbs)
