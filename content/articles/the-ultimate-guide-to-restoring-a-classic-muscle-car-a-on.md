## Step-by-Step Guide
The restoration process for a classic muscle car like the 1969 Dodge Charger Daytona is a complex and time-consuming task, requiring a range of skills and expertise.

### Engine Rebuild
The engine rebuild is a critical part of the restoration process, requiring careful attention to detail and a range of specialized tools.

#### Step 1: Disassemble the Engine
* Remove the engine from the vehicle and disassemble the components, including the cylinder head, pistons, and crankshaft
* Inspect the components for wear or damage, and replace as necessary

#### Step 2: Machine the Engine Block
* Use a machine shop to bore and hone the engine block, ensuring a smooth and precise surface
* Install new piston rings and bearings, and reassemble the engine

### Transmission Rebuild
The transmission rebuild is another critical part of the restoration process, requiring careful attention to detail and a range of specialized tools.

#### Step 1: Disassemble the Transmission
* Remove the transmission from the vehicle and disassemble the components, including the gearset, bearings, and seals
* Inspect the components for wear or damage, and replace as necessary

#### Step 2: Rebuild the Transmission
* Use a transmission rebuild kit to replace the worn or damaged components, including the gearset, bearings, and seals
* Reassemble the transmission, ensuring that all components are properly aligned and secured

### Suspension and Brake Overhaul
The suspension and brake overhaul is a critical part of the restoration process, requiring careful attention to detail and a range of specialized tools.

#### Step 1: Inspect the Suspension and Brakes
* Inspect the suspension and brakes for wear or damage, including the shocks, springs, and brake pads
* Replace any worn or damaged components, including the shocks, springs, and brake pads

#### Step 2: Rebuild the Suspension and Brakes
* Use a suspension and brake rebuild kit to replace the worn or damaged components, including the shocks, springs, and brake pads
* Reassemble the suspension and brakes, ensuring that all components are properly aligned and secured

## Pro Tips & Common Mistakes
When restoring a classic muscle car like the 1969 Dodge Charger Daytona, it's essential to avoid common mistakes and follow proven techniques.

* Don't skip the engine rebuild, as this can lead to premature wear and damage
* Use high-quality components and materials, including piston rings, bearings, and seals
* Ensure that all components are properly aligned and secured, including the engine, transmission, and suspension
