## Step-by-Step Guide
### Introduction to Audi RS Q5 Maintenance
The Audi RS Q5 requires regular maintenance to ensure optimal performance and longevity. This includes routine oil changes, tire rotations, and brake pad replacements.

#### Oil Change Procedure
1. Locate the oil drain plug under the engine and position a drain pan to catch the oil.
2. Use a 10mm socket to remove the oil drain plug and allow the oil to drain completely.
3. Replace the oil drain plug and tighten to the specified torque (30 Nm).
4. Fill the engine with the recommended oil type and capacity (approximately 6 quarts).

#### Brake Pad Replacement Procedure
1. Raise the vehicle on a lift or jack stands to access the brake calipers.
2. Remove the wheel and caliper to access the brake pads.
3. Use a brake pad spreader to push the piston back into the caliper.
4. Remove the old brake pads and install the new ones, making sure to align the wear sensor correctly.

#### Suspension Inspection Procedure
1. Raise the vehicle on a lift or jack stands to access the suspension components.
2. Inspect the shock absorbers, springs, and control arms for any signs of wear or damage.
3. Use a torque wrench to check the tightness of the suspension bolts and nuts.

## Pro Tips & Common Mistakes
When working on the Audi RS Q5, avoid using the wrong type of oil or exceeding the recommended tire pressure. Additionally, never neglect routine maintenance tasks, such as oil changes and brake pad replacements.

Tricks of the trade include using a torque wrench to tighten bolts and nuts to the specified torque, and using a brake pad spreader to make brake pad replacement easier.

The cost of maintaining the Audi RS Q5 can vary depending on the task and whether you choose to DIY or take it to a mechanic. ## Frequently Asked Questions
1. **What type of oil should I use in my Audi RS Q5?**
The recommended oil type for the Audi RS Q5 is 5W-40 synthetic oil.
2. **How often should I change my brake pads?**
Brake pads should be replaced every 30,000-50,000 miles, depending on driving conditions.
3. **Can I use aftermarket parts on my Audi RS Q5?**
While aftermarket parts may be available, it's recommended to use only genuine Audi parts to ensure compatibility and warranty validity.
4. **How do I reset the maintenance reminder on my Audi RS Q5?**
The maintenance reminder can be reset through the vehicle's infotainment system or by consulting the owner's manual.
5. **What is the recommended tire pressure for the Audi RS Q5?**
The recommended tire pressure for the Audi RS Q5 is 35-40 psi, depending on the driving conditions and load.
