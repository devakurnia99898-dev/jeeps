## Step-by-Step Guide
In this section, we'll provide a detailed step-by-step guide for common maintenance tasks and repairs on the 2027 Chrysler Pacifica.

### Oil Change
#### Step 1: Gather materials
* Oil filter wrench
* New oil filter
* Oil drain pan
* New oil
#### Step 2: Locate the oil drain plug
* The oil drain plug is located at the bottom of the engine, near the front of the vehicle.
* Use a 10mm socket to remove the plug.
#### Step 3: Drain the oil
* Allow the oil to drain into the oil drain pan.
* Replace the oil drain plug and tighten to 30 ft-lbs.

### Brake Pad Replacement
#### Step 1: Remove the wheel
* Use a lug wrench to loosen the lug nuts.
* Raise the vehicle using a jack and support it with jack stands.
#### Step 2: Remove the caliper
* Use a 10mm socket to remove the caliper bolts.
* Slide the caliper off the rotor.
#### Step 3: Remove the old brake pads
* Use a brake pad spreader to compress the caliper piston.
* Remove the old brake pads and discard.
