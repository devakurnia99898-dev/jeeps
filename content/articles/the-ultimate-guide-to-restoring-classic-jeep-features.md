## Technical Deep Dive
The modern Jeep interior is dominated by touchscreens and digital interfaces. While these systems offer a range of benefits, including improved functionality and connectivity, they often lack the tactile feedback and simplicity of classic buttons and knobs.

One of the most common failure points in modern Jeep interiors is the touchscreen itself. The plastic screen can be prone to scratches and cracks, and the underlying electronics can be sensitive to water and dust. In contrast, classic buttons and knobs are often more durable and easier to repair.

## Step-by-Step Guide
### Restore Classic Buttons
#### Step 1: Remove the Trim Panel
To access the buttons, you'll need to remove the trim panel. Use a 10mm socket to remove the screws, then gently pry the panel away from the dashboard.

#### Step 2: Disconnect the Wiring
Use wire strippers to disconnect the wiring from the old buttons. Take note of the color-coding and wiring diagram to ensure you connect the new buttons correctly.

#### Step 3: Install the New Buttons
Install the new buttons, making sure to secure them firmly to the dashboard. Use a torque wrench to tighten the screws to the recommended specification (10-15 Nm).

### Install Classic Knobs
#### Step 1: Remove the Old Knob
Use a Phillips screwdriver to remove the screw holding the old knob in place. Gently pull the knob away from the dashboard.

#### Step 2: Install the New Knob
Install the new knob, making sure to secure it firmly to the dashboard. Use a torque wrench to tighten the screw to the recommended specification (5-10 Nm).

#### Step 3: Connect the Wiring
Use wire strippers to connect the wiring to the new knob. Take note of the color-coding and wiring diagram to ensure you connect the wiring correctly.

## Pro Tips & Common Mistakes
* Don't overtighten the screws, as this can damage the dashboard or buttons
* Use a torque wrench to ensure the correct tightening specification
* Take your time when disconnecting and reconnecting the wiring to avoid damaging the electrical system
