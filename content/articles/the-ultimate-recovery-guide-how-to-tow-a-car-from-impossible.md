## Technical Deep Dive
The recovery process involves several key components, including:
- Winching: A winch is a device used to pull the vehicle to safety. There are several types of winches, including electric, hydraulic, and manual.
- Snatch blocks: Snatch blocks are used to change the direction of the winch cable and increase the mechanical advantage.
- Recovery straps: Recovery straps are used to connect the vehicle to the winch or another vehicle.
- Anchors: Anchors are used to secure the winch or recovery strap to a stable object.

Common failure points in the recovery process include:
- Insufficient anchor points
- Incorrect use of recovery equipment
- Inadequate planning and preparation
