# **Technical Deep Dive**
License plate recognition (LPR) systems use a combination of cameras, software, and databases to identify and track vehicles. The process involves:
* Camera capture: High-resolution cameras capture images of license plates
* Image processing: Software enhances and clarifies the images
* Character recognition: Optical character recognition (OCR) technology extracts the license plate number and other relevant information
* Database matching: The extracted information is matched against a database of known license plates

Common failure points in LPR systems include:
* Poor camera quality or positioning
* Inadequate lighting or weather conditions
* Incorrect or outdated database information
