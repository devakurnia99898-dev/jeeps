## Step-by-Step Guide
### Step 1: Planning and Design
#### Step 1.1: Determine the scope of the project
Before starting any customization project, it's essential to determine the scope of the project. This includes deciding which areas of the vehicle to modify and what type of modifications to make.
#### Step 1.2: Research and gather inspiration
Research different customization options and gather inspiration from other vehicles. This can include looking at pictures of customized Mini Coopers, reading forums and blogs, and talking to other car enthusiasts.
#### Step 1.3: Create a budget and plan
Create a budget and plan for the project, including the cost of materials, tools, and labor. It's essential to prioritize the modifications and focus on the most important aspects of the project.

#### Step 2.3: Install a spoiler

## Pro Tips & Common Mistakes
* Always follow the manufacturer's instructions when installing any modifications.
* Ensure that all modifications are compatible with the vehicle's systems and do not compromise its performance or safety.
* Don't over-modify the vehicle, as this can lead to reliability issues and decreased performance.
