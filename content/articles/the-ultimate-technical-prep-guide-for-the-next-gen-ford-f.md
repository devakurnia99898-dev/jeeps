## Step-by-Step Guide
### Engine Overview
The next-gen F-150 and Super Duty will likely feature advanced engine technologies, including improved EcoBoost engines. To prepare for these new engines, it's essential to understand the current engine specifications and capabilities.

#### Engine Disassembly
To access the engine components, follow these steps:
1. Remove the engine cover and intake manifold
2. Disconnect the fuel injectors and spark plugs
3. Remove the turbocharger and exhaust manifold

#### Engine Reassembly
Once the engine components have been accessed, follow these steps to reassemble:
1. Reconnect the fuel injectors and spark plugs
2. Reinstall the turbocharger and exhaust manifold
3. Reconnect the intake manifold and engine cover

### Suspension System
The suspension system in the next-gen F-150 and Super Duty may feature advanced shock absorbers and stabilizer bars. To prepare for these upgrades, it's essential to understand the current suspension system and its components.

#### Suspension Disassembly
To access the suspension components, follow these steps:
1. Remove the wheels and brake calipers
2. Disconnect the shock absorbers and stabilizer bars
3. Remove the suspension springs and control arms

#### Suspension Reassembly
Once the suspension components have been accessed, follow these steps to reassemble:
1. Reconnect the shock absorbers and stabilizer bars
2. Reinstall the suspension springs and control arms
3. Reconnect the brake calipers and wheels

### Electrical System
