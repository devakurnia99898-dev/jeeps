## Frequently Asked Questions
1. **What is the recommended oil type for the 2027 Hyundai Elantra?**
The recommended oil type for the 2027 Hyundai Elantra is 0W-20 synthetic oil.
2. **How often should I rotate the tires on my 2027 Hyundai Elantra?**
It's recommended to rotate the tires every 5,000 to 7,500 miles to ensure even wear and prolong their lifespan.
3. **Can I update the infotainment system myself?**
Yes, you can update the infotainment system yourself using the update software provided by Hyundai. However, it's recommended to consult a professional if you're unsure or uncomfortable with the process.
4. **What are the symptoms of worn-out brake pads?**
Symptoms of worn-out brake pads include squealing or grinding noises, vibration, and reduced braking performance.
5. **How often should I inspect the brake pads on my 2027 Hyundai Elantra?**
It's recommended to inspect the brake pads every 10,000 to 15,000 miles or as specified in the owner's manual.
