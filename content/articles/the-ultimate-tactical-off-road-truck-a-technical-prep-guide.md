## Step-by-Step Guide
### Suspension System Modification
The suspension system of the Rezvani Fortress can be modified to improve its off-road capability. This can be achieved by installing aftermarket coil springs, shock absorbers, and control arms.

#### Coil Spring Installation
1. Remove the wheel and brake caliper to access the coil spring.
2. Use a spring compressor to compress the coil spring.
3. Remove the coil spring and replace it with the aftermarket unit.
4. Reinstall the coil spring and tighten the spring compressor.

#### Shock Absorber Installation
1. Remove the shock absorber from the vehicle.
2. Inspect the shock absorber for any signs of wear or damage.
3. Install the aftermarket shock absorber, ensuring proper alignment and tightening.

#### Control Arm Installation
1. Remove the control arm from the vehicle.
2. Inspect the control arm for any signs of wear or damage.
3. Install the aftermarket control arm, ensuring proper alignment and tightening.
