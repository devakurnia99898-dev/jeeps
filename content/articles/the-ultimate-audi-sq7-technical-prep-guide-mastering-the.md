Common failure points in the SQ7's system include the turbochargers, which can be prone to failure due to excessive heat and stress. ## Step-by-Step Guide
### Step 1: Pre-Maintenance Checks
Before performing any maintenance or repairs on the SQ7, it's essential to conduct a series of pre-maintenance checks to ensure the vehicle is in good working condition.
#### Step 1.1: Check the Oil and Fluid Levels
Check the engine oil, transmission fluid, and coolant levels to ensure they are at the recommended levels.
#### Step 1.2: Inspect the Tires and Brakes
Inspect the tires for any signs of wear or damage and check the brake pads for any signs of wear.

### Step 2: Suspension System Maintenance
The SQ7's suspension system requires regular maintenance to ensure optimal performance and handling.
#### Step 2.1: Check the Air Suspension System
Check the air suspension system for any signs of leaks or damage and ensure the system is functioning properly.
#### Step 2.2: Inspect the Shocks and Struts
Inspect the shocks and struts for any signs of wear or damage and replace them if necessary.

### Step 3: Engine and Transmission Maintenance
The SQ7's engine and transmission require regular maintenance to ensure optimal performance and longevity.
#### Step 3.1: Check the Engine Oil and Filter
Check the engine oil and filter and replace them if necessary.
#### Step 3.2: Inspect the Timing Chain and Water Pump
Inspect the timing chain and water pump for any signs of wear or damage and replace them if necessary.

## Pro Tips & Common Mistakes
When working on the SQ7, it's essential to avoid common mistakes, such as over-tightening the suspension system or neglecting to check the engine oil and filter regularly.
