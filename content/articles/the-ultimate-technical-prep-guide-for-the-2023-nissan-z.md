The engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission, which provides smooth and precise shifting. The suspension system is designed to provide optimal handling and stability, with a double-wishbone front suspension and a multi-link rear suspension.

Common failure points in the 2023 Nissan Z include the turbochargers, which can be prone to overheating and failure if not properly maintained. The transmission is also a critical component, and should be regularly inspected and maintained to prevent issues.

## Step-by-Step Guide
### Step 1: Pre-Purchase Inspection
#### Step 1.1: Exterior Inspection
Inspect the exterior of the vehicle for any signs of damage or wear, including dents, scratches, or rust. Check the tires for uneven wear or damage, and ensure the wheels are properly aligned.

#### Step 1.2: Interior Inspection
Inspect the interior of the vehicle for any signs of wear or damage, including tears or stains on the seats or carpets. Check the dashboard and instrument cluster for any warning lights or issues.

### Step 2: Maintenance Schedule
#### Step 2.1: Oil Change
Change the oil every 5,000 miles, using a high-quality synthetic oil that meets the manufacturer's specifications. Use a torque wrench to tighten the oil drain plug to the specified torque.

#### Step 2.2: Tire Rotation
Rotate the tires every 5,000 miles, using a tire rotation pattern that ensures even wear. Check the tire pressure and adjust as needed.

### Step 3: Performance Upgrades
#### Step 3.1: Engine Tuning
Consider upgrading the engine with a performance tune, which can increase horsepower and torque. This should be done by a qualified mechanic or tuner.

#### Step 3.2: Suspension Upgrades
Consider upgrading the suspension with high-performance coilovers or sway bars, which can improve handling and stability. This should be done by a qualified mechanic or suspension specialist.

## Pro Tips & Common Mistakes
* Always follow the manufacturer's recommended maintenance schedule to prevent premature wear and tear.
* Avoid over-revving the engine, as this can cause damage to the turbochargers or other components.
* Consider investing in a high-quality air filter, which can improve engine performance and efficiency.
