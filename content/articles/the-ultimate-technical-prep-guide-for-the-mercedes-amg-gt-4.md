# Step-by-Step Guide
### Technical Inspection
The first step in preparing your Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door is to perform a thorough technical inspection. This includes checking the electric motor, battery management system, and power electronics for any signs of damage or wear.

#### Visual Inspection
* Inspect the electric motor for any signs of damage or wear, including cracks, dents, or corrosion.
* Check the battery management system for any signs of damage or wear, including cracks, dents, or corrosion.
* Inspect the power electronics for any signs of damage or wear, including cracks, dents, or corrosion.

#### Electrical System Check
* Use a multimeter to check the electrical system for any signs of damage or wear, including voltage drops, resistance issues, or short circuits.
* Use a scan tool to check the vehicle's computer system for any error codes or issues.

### Suspension Maintenance
The next step is to perform suspension maintenance, including checking and replacing the shock absorbers, springs, and bushings as needed.

#### Shock Absorber Check
* Inspect the shock absorbers for any signs of damage or wear, including leaks, dents, or corrosion.
* Check the shock absorbers for proper function, including compression and rebound.

#### Spring Check
* Inspect the springs for any signs of damage or wear, including cracks, dents, or corrosion.
* Check the springs for proper function, including compression and rebound.

### Electrical System Check
The final step is to perform an electrical system check, including checking the high-voltage electrical system, advanced infotainment system, and sophisticated driver assistance systems.

#### High-Voltage Electrical System Check
