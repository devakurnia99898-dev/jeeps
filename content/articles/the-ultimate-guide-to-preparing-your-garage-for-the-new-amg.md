## Step-by-Step Guide
### Step 1: Prepare Your Garage
The first step in preparing your garage for the new Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door is to ensure it's equipped with the necessary infrastructure. This includes installing a high-voltage charging station, which can supply the vehicle with the required 240V or 480V power.
#### Sub-step 1.1: Assess Your Electrical System
Before installing a charging station, assess your garage's electrical system to determine if it can handle the increased power demand. You may need to upgrade your electrical panel or install a dedicated 240V or 480V circuit.
#### Sub-step 1.2: Choose a Charging Station
Select a charging station that's compatible with the Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door and meets your specific needs. Consider factors such as charging speed, power output, and connectivity options.

### Step 2: Install a Charging Station
Once you've selected a charging station, follow the manufacturer's instructions to install it in your garage. Ensure the station is properly grounded and connected to the electrical system.
#### Sub-step 2.1: Mount the Charging Station
Mount the charging station to a wall or floor, following the manufacturer's guidelines. Ensure the station is securely fastened and protected from the elements.
#### Sub-step 2.2: Connect the Charging Station
Connect the charging station to the electrical system, following the manufacturer's instructions. Ensure all connections are secure and meet local electrical codes.

### Step 3: Prepare Your Toolbox
In addition to the necessary tools, it's essential to have a well-stocked toolbox with the required equipment to maintain and repair the Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door.

## Pro Tips & Common Mistakes
* Always follow the manufacturer's instructions when working with electric vehicles.
* Ensure proper safety precautions are taken when working with high-voltage systems.
* Don't attempt to repair or maintain the vehicle without proper training and experience.
* Regularly inspect and maintain the charging station to ensure optimal performance and safety.
