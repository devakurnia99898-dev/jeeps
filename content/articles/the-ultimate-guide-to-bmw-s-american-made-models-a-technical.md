## Step-by-Step Guide
### Step 1: Oil Change
#### Step 1.1: Locate the Oil Drain Plug
Find the oil drain plug located at the bottom of the engine. Use a 10mm socket to remove the plug.
#### Step 1.2: Drain the Oil
Allow the oil to drain into a pan. Replace the oil drain plug and tighten it to the specified torque (30 ft-lbs).
#### Step 1.3: Replace the Oil Filter
Use an oil filter wrench to remove the old oil filter. Install a new oil filter and hand tighten it.

### Step 2: Brake Pad Replacement
#### Step 2.1: Remove the Wheels
Remove the wheels to access the brake calipers. Use a 12mm socket to loosen the lug nuts.
#### Step 2.2: Remove the Caliper
Use a brake pad spreader to push the caliper piston back. Remove the caliper and take out the old brake pads.
#### Step 2.3: Install New Brake Pads
Install the new brake pads and reassemble the caliper. Tighten the caliper bolts to the specified torque (25 ft-lbs).

### Step 3: Fuel Pump Replacement
#### Step 3.1: Locate the Fuel Pump
Find the fuel pump located in the fuel tank. Use a 10mm socket to remove the fuel pump mounting bolts.
#### Step 3.2: Disconnect the Fuel Lines
Disconnect the fuel lines from the fuel pump. Use a fuel line wrench to loosen the fuel line fittings.
#### Step 3.3: Install the New Fuel Pump
Install the new fuel pump and reconnect the fuel lines. Tighten the fuel pump mounting bolts to the specified torque (15 ft-lbs).

## Pro Tips & Common Mistakes
* Always use the correct type of oil for your BMW model.
* Avoid over-tightening the oil drain plug, as it can damage the engine block.
* Use a torque wrench to ensure accurate tightening of bolts and nuts.
