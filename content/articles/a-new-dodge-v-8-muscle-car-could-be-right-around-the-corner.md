## Introduction to Jeep Off-Road Vehicles
The Jeep Wrangler Rubicon is a quintessential off-road vehicle, boasting a rugged design and impressive capabilities. Its **3.6L V6 24V VVT engine** produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, making it well-suited for tackling challenging terrain.

#### Suspension Geometry
The Jeep Wrangler Rubicon features a **heavy-duty Dana 44 HD front axle** and a **Dana 44 HD rear axle**, providing a robust foundation for its off-road suspension. The vehicle's **5-link coil suspension** allows for excellent articulation and stability, making it well-suited for navigating rocky and uneven terrain.

## Visual Description of a Jeep Vehicle
A Jeep Wrangler Rubicon is characterized by its **boxy design**, **round headlights**, and **seven-slot grille**. Its **removable doors** and **fold-down windshield** provide an open-air driving experience, while its **33-inch all-terrain tires** offer ample traction and ground clearance.
