## Technical Deep Dive
The Ram 1500's 5.7L V8 engine is a powerful unit, producing 395 horsepower and 410 lb-ft of torque. Roush Performance's upgrades are likely to focus on enhancing the engine's performance, suspension, and exhaust system. The engine's performance can be further improved by upgrading the air intake, exhaust system, and tuning the engine computer. The suspension system can be upgraded with high-performance shocks, springs, and sway bars to improve handling and stability.

Common failure points in the Ram 1500 include the water pump, alternator, and engine mounts. The water pump's plastic impeller can fail, causing overheating issues. The alternator's bearings can wear out, leading to electrical system malfunctions. The engine mounts can deteriorate, causing vibrations and noise.
