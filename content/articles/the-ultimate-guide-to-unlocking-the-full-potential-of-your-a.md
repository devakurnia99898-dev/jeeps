## Technical Deep Dive
The Toyota Supra's engine is a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6, producing 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. While these numbers may seem impressive, they can be improved upon with the right modifications. The engine's turbocharger, fuel system, and exhaust system are all areas that can be upgraded to increase power output.

Common failure points in the Supra's engine include the turbocharger's wastegate, the fuel pump, and the exhaust system's catalytic converter. These components can be upgraded or replaced with high-performance alternatives to increase power and reliability.

## Pro Tips & Common Mistakes
* Don't over-tighten bolts and fasteners, as this can damage threads and strip heads.
* Use proper safety equipment, including gloves and safety glasses, when working with power tools and welding equipment.
* Never work on a hot engine or exhaust system, as this can cause serious injury.
