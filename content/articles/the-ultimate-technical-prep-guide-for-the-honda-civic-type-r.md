## Pro Tips & Common Mistakes
When working on the Honda Civic Type R, it's essential to avoid common mistakes, such as:
* Over-tightening bolts and fasteners, which can damage threads or strip heads
* Under-tightening bolts and fasteners, which can lead to loose connections and decreased performance
* Ignoring safety precautions, which can result in injury or damage to the vehicle

Pro tips include:
* Using high-quality tools and materials to ensure proper fitment and performance
* Following proper torque specifications to avoid over- or under-tightening
* Consulting with a professional mechanic or engineer if unsure about any aspect of the upgrade process
