The engine will be paired with a six-speed manual transmission or a dual-clutch automatic transmission. The suspension system will likely feature a MacPherson strut setup at the front and a torsion beam at the rear.

Common failure points in the Fiat Grizzly's engine and suspension systems may include the turbocharger, which can be prone to failure due to excessive heat or oil contamination. The suspension system's bushings and bearings can also wear out over time, causing vibrations and uneven tire wear.
