## Step-by-Step Guide
### Preparation and Inspection
The first step in preparing the Red Bull hypercar for maintenance and optimization is to conduct a thorough inspection of the vehicle. This includes checking the engine, suspension, and electrical systems for any signs of wear or damage.

#### Visual Inspection
Start by visually inspecting the engine, suspension, and electrical systems for any signs of wear or damage. Check for any leaks, corrosion, or damage to components.

#### Diagnostic Scan
Next, perform a diagnostic scan using a scan tool to check for any trouble codes or issues with the vehicle's systems.

#### Fluid Check
Check the engine oil, transmission fluid, brake fluid, and coolant levels to ensure they are at the recommended levels.

### Maintenance and Optimization
Once the inspection is complete, it's time to perform routine maintenance and optimization tasks to ensure the Red Bull hypercar is running at its best.

#### Oil Change
Perform an oil change using high-performance synthetic oil to ensure the engine is properly lubricated.

#### Filter Replacement
Replace the air filter, fuel filter, and cabin air filter to ensure the engine is getting the air and fuel it needs to run efficiently.

#### Suspension Adjustment
Adjust the suspension to the recommended settings to ensure optimal handling and performance.

#### Brake Pad Replacement
Replace the brake pads with high-performance pads to ensure the vehicle can stop quickly and safely.
