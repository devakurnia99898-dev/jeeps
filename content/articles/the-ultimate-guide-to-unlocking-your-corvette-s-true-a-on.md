## Technical Deep Dive
The ECM is the brain of the Corvette's engine, controlling everything from fuel injection to ignition timing. It is a complex system that relies on a multitude of sensors and actuators to optimize engine performance. However, the ECM is also heavily protected by the manufacturer, making it difficult for tuners to access and modify.

The process of unlocking the ECM involves cracking the code that controls the engine's performance parameters. This requires a deep understanding of the engine's mechanics, as well as the software and hardware that control it. Common failure points in the ECM include:

* The fuel injection system
* The ignition system
* The engine's sensors and actuators
