One of the key components of the ECM is the engine's fuel injection system. The fuel injectors are responsible for delivering the precise amount of fuel to the engine's cylinders, and the ECM controls the fuel injectors to ensure optimal engine performance. However, the ECM's fuel injection mapping is limited by the manufacturer's calibration, which can restrict the engine's power output.

Another key component of the ECM is the engine's ignition system.
