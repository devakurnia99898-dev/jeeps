## Step-by-Step Guide
### Prepare the Garage and Tools
#### Clear the Workspace
Clear a large, well-ventilated area of your garage to accommodate the Zenvo Aurora, ensuring easy access to all sides of the vehicle.
#### Gather Tools and Equipment
Collect all necessary tools and equipment, including sockets, wrenches, pliers, and diagnostic devices.
### Inspect the Vehicle
#### Visual Inspection
Perform a thorough visual inspection of the vehicle, looking for signs of wear, damage, or corrosion on the exterior and interior.
#### Diagnostic Scan
Connect a diagnostic device to the vehicle's OBD-II port to scan for any trouble codes or issues with the engine, transmission, or other systems.
### Prepare the Engine
#### Oil and Fluid Check
Check the engine oil, coolant, and brake fluid levels, topping them off as necessary.
#### Filter Replacement
Replace the engine air filter, oil filter, and fuel filter to ensure optimal performance and prevent potential issues.
### Prepare the Suspension and Brakes
#### Suspension Inspection
Inspect the coilovers, sway bars, and bushings for signs of wear or damage, replacing them if necessary.
#### Brake Pad Replacement
Replace the brake pads and rotors if they show signs of excessive wear, ensuring optimal braking performance.
### Final Check and Test Drive
#### Final Inspection
Perform a final inspection of the vehicle, checking for any loose connections, leaks, or other issues.
#### Test Drive
Take the Zenvo Aurora for a test drive to ensure all systems are functioning properly and make any necessary adjustments.

## Pro Tips & Common Mistakes
Don't overlook the importance of regular maintenance and inspections to prevent potential issues with the Zenvo Aurora. Common mistakes include neglecting to check the engine oil and coolant levels, failing to replace worn-out brake pads, and ignoring unusual noises or vibrations. Tricks of the trade include using a torque wrench to ensure accurate tightening of bolts and fasteners, and keeping a detailed record of maintenance and repairs to track the vehicle's history.
