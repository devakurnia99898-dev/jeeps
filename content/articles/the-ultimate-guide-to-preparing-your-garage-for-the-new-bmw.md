## Step-by-Step Guide
In this section, we'll take a step-by-step approach to preparing your garage for the new BMW 3 Series Touring.
### Step 1: Gather Tools and Equipment
The first step in preparing your garage is to gather the necessary tools and equipment. This includes a range of sockets, wrenches, pliers, and screwdrivers. You'll also need a torque wrench and a set of jack stands to support the vehicle.
#### Step 1.1: Inspect Your Toolbox
Take a close look at your toolbox and ensure that you have all the necessary tools and equipment. Make a list of any items that are missing or need to be replaced.
#### Step 1.2: Purchase Additional Tools
If you're missing any tools or equipment, now is the time to purchase them. Consider investing in a high-quality socket set and a torque wrench.
### Step 2: Prepare Your Garage
The next step is to prepare your garage for the new vehicle. This includes clearing out any clutter, cleaning the floor, and ensuring that the garage is well-lit.
#### Step 2.1: Clear Out Clutter
Take the time to clear out any clutter or debris from your garage. This will give you a clean and safe workspace to work on your vehicle.
#### Step 2.2: Clean the Floor
Clean the floor of your garage to ensure that it's free from dirt and debris. This will help prevent any damage to the vehicle's underside.
### Step 3: Install a Lift or Jack Stands
If you plan on doing any serious work on your BMW 3 Series Touring, you'll need to install a lift or jack stands. This will give you the clearance and support you need to work under the vehicle.
#### Step 3.1: Choose a Lift or Jack Stands
Decide whether you want to install a lift or use jack stands. Consider the cost and convenience of each option.
#### Step 3.2: Install the Lift or Jack Stands
Follow the manufacturer's instructions to install the lift or jack stands. Ensure that they are properly secured and level.
