## Step-by-Step Guide
### Prepare Your Garage
The first step in preparing your garage for the Lamborghini Revuelto is to ensure adequate lighting. Install high-intensity LED lights to illuminate the work area and provide sufficient visibility.

#### Install a Lift or Jack
A lift or jack is essential for working on the Revuelto's underside. Choose a high-quality lift or jack specifically designed for exotic vehicles.

#### Organize Your Tools
Invest in a tool chest or cabinet to store your specialized tools and equipment. Label each tool and organize them by category (e.g., electrical, mechanical).

#### Install a Battery Management System Tester
The Revuelto's hybrid powertrain requires a specialized battery management system tester. Install this equipment in your garage to ensure proper maintenance and diagnosis.

### Maintain Your Revuelto
Regular maintenance is crucial to ensure the Revuelto's optimal performance. Schedule regular inspections and maintenance sessions with a qualified Lamborghini technician.

#### Check the Battery Management System
Use the battery management system tester to monitor the Revuelto's battery health and performance. Address any issues promptly to prevent damage to the electrical system.

#### Inspect the Aerodynamic Components
Regularly inspect the Revuelto's aerodynamic components, including the active spoilers and air intakes. Clean and maintain these components to ensure optimal performance.

#### Update the Software
The Revuelto's advanced systems require regular software updates. Ensure you have the latest software installed to maintain optimal performance and security.
