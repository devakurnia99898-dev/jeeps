## Step-by-Step Guide
### Step 1: Oil Change
The oil change is one of the most critical maintenance tasks for the Alpina XB7's engine. To perform an oil change, follow these steps:
#### Step 1.1: Warm up the engine
Drive the vehicle for a few minutes to warm up the engine. This will help the oil drain more easily.
#### Step 1.2: Locate the oil drain plug
Underneath the vehicle, you'll find the oil drain plug located near the front of the engine. Use a 10mm socket to remove the plug.
#### Step 1.3: Drain the oil
Allow the oil to drain completely into a pan before replacing the plug. Tighten the plug to 30 ft-lbs.
#### Step 1.4: Replace the oil filter
Use a filter wrench to remove the oil filter. Dispose of the old filter responsibly and replace it with a new one.
#### Step 1.5: Refill the oil
Use a high-quality synthetic oil that meets the manufacturer's specifications. Fill the engine with the recommended amount of oil.
### Step 2: Spark Plug Replacement
Spark plug replacement is another critical maintenance task for the Alpina XB7's engine. To replace the spark plugs, follow these steps:
#### Step 2.1: Locate the spark plugs
The spark plugs are located on the side of the engine, near the cylinder head. Use a spark plug socket to remove the plugs.
#### Step 2.2: Remove the spark plugs
Use a spark plug socket to remove the spark plugs. Be careful not to touch the spark plug wires to avoid damaging the engine.
#### Step 2.3: Inspect the spark plugs
Inspect the spark plugs for signs of wear or damage. If the spark plugs are damaged, replace them with new ones.
#### Step 2.4: Install the new spark plugs
Use a spark plug socket to install the new spark plugs. Tighten the plugs to 15 ft-lbs.
### Step 3: Turbocharger Inspection
The turbochargers are a critical component of the Alpina XB7's engine. To inspect the turbochargers, follow these steps:
#### Step 3.1: Locate the turbochargers
The turbochargers are located on the side of the engine, near the exhaust manifold. Use a 12mm socket to remove the turbocharger housing.
#### Step 3.2: Inspect the turbochargers
Inspect the turbochargers for signs of wear or damage. If the turbochargers are damaged, replace them with new ones.
#### Step 3.3: Replace the turbocharger housing
Use a 12mm socket to replace the turbocharger housing. Tighten the housing to 20 ft-lbs.

## Pro Tips & Common Mistakes
When maintaining the Alpina XB7's engine, there are a number of common mistakes to avoid. One of the most common mistakes is over-tightening the spark plugs, which can damage the engine. To avoid these mistakes, always follow the manufacturer's recommendations and use high-quality parts and materials.
