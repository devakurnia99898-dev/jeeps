## Unleashing the Beast
The wait is over for Jeep enthusiasts as the V-8 Jeep Wrangler is back for 2026, and it's more affordable than ever. Jeep is offering the powerful engine in a limited-edition Wrangler Moab 392, priced at $20,000 less than last year's model.

## Key Features and Specs
The 2026 Jeep Wrangler Moab 392 comes with a range of impressive features, including:
| Feature | Specification |
| --- | --- |
| Engine | 6.4L V8 |
| Horsepower | 470 hp |
| Torque | 470 lb-ft |
| Transmission | 8-speed automatic |
| Drive Type | Four-wheel drive |
| Ground Clearance | 10.3 inches |
| Approach/Departure Angles | 43.8/35.6 degrees |

## Tackling the Trails
With its powerful V8 engine and impressive ground clearance, the Jeep Wrangler Moab 392 is designed to tackle even the toughest off-road trails. The vehicle's four-wheel-drive system and 33-inch all-terrain tires provide the necessary traction and control to navigate rocky terrain and steep inclines.

### Off-Road Capability
The Jeep Wrangler Moab 392 is built for off-road enthusiasts who demand a vehicle that can keep up with their adventurous lifestyle. Whether you're crawling over rocks or speeding through the desert, this vehicle has the power and capability to deliver an unforgettable driving experience.

With its reduced price point and impressive specs, the 2026 Jeep Wrangler Moab 392 is an attractive option for anyone looking to upgrade their off-road game. Get ready to unleash the beast and take on the toughest trails in style.
