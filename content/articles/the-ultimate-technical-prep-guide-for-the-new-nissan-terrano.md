## Step-by-Step Guide
### Step 1: Preparing the Engine
#### Step 1.1: Oil Change
Change the engine oil to ensure the vehicle is running smoothly. Use a high-quality oil filter and fill the engine with the recommended type and amount of oil.
#### Step 1.2: Air Filter Replacement
Replace the air filter to improve engine performance and fuel efficiency. Use a high-quality air filter and follow the manufacturer's instructions for installation.
### Step 2: Upgrading the Suspension
#### Step 2.1: Coil Spring Replacement
Replace the coil springs with heavy-duty springs to improve the vehicle's off-road capability. Use a spring compressor to compress the springs and remove the old ones.
#### Step 2.2: Shock Absorber Upgrade
Upgrade the shock absorbers to heavy-duty shock absorbers to improve the vehicle's stability and handling. Use a shock absorber compressor to compress the shock absorbers and remove the old ones.
### Step 3: Electrical System Upgrades
