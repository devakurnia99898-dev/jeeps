## Step-by-Step Guide
### Checking and Maintaining the Battery
The battery is a critical component of the BMW iX, and regular checks can help extend its lifespan. To check the battery, follow these steps:
#### Step 1: Locate the Battery
The battery is located in the floorpan of the vehicle, and access is gained through the trunk.
#### Step 2: Check the Battery Voltage
Use a multimeter to check the battery voltage, which should be between 350-400V.
#### Step 3: Check the Battery Temperature
Use a thermometer to check the battery temperature, which should be between 20-40°C.

### Updating the Software
Regular software updates can help improve the performance and functionality of the BMW iX. To update the software, follow these steps:
#### Step 1: Connect the Vehicle to a Computer
Use a software update cable to connect the vehicle to a computer.
#### Step 2: Download the Latest Software
Download the latest software from the BMW website.
#### Step 3: Install the Software Update
Follow the on-screen instructions to install the software update.

### Replacing the Brake Pads
The brake pads on the BMW iX should be replaced every 30,000 miles or as needed. To replace the brake pads, follow these steps:
#### Step 1: Jack Up the Vehicle
Use a jack to lift the vehicle and remove the wheels.
#### Step 2: Remove the Caliper
Use a 10mm socket to remove the caliper bolts.
#### Step 3: Remove the Old Brake Pads
Pull out the old brake pads and discard them.
#### Step 4: Install the New Brake Pads
Install the new brake pads and replace the caliper.
