## Step-by-Step Guide
### Step 1: Inspecting the Suspension System
The first step in preparing for the new Renegade is to inspect the current model's suspension system. This includes checking the condition of the shock absorbers, springs, and control arms.
#### Step 1.1: Checking the Shock Absorbers
To check the shock absorbers, start by lifting the vehicle and removing the wheels. Then, use a wrench to loosen the shock absorber's mounting bolt. Next, use a shock absorber test tool to check the shock's compression and rebound.
#### Step 1.2: Checking the Springs
To check the springs, start by measuring the ride height of the vehicle. Then, use a spring compressor to check the spring's tension.
#### Step 1.3: Checking the Control Arms
To check the control arms, start by inspecting the ball joints and bushings. Then, use a wrench to check the control arm's mounting bolts.
### Step 2: Upgrading the Suspension System
Once the inspection is complete, it's time to upgrade the suspension system. This includes replacing the shock absorbers, springs, and control arms with high-performance components.
#### Step 2.1: Replacing the Shock Absorbers
To replace the shock absorbers, start by removing the old shock absorbers and installing the new ones. Then, use a wrench to tighten the shock absorber's mounting bolt to the specified torque (35 Nm).
#### Step 2.2: Replacing the Springs
To replace the springs, start by removing the old springs and installing the new ones. Then, use a spring compressor to compress the spring to the specified tension (100 Nm).
#### Step 2.3: Replacing the Control Arms
To replace the control arms, start by removing the old control arms and installing the new ones. Then, use a wrench to tighten the control arm's mounting bolts to the specified torque (40 Nm).
### Step 3: Tuning the Engine
The final step in preparing for the new Renegade is to tune the engine. This includes upgrading the air intake, exhaust system, and engine computer.
#### Step 3.1: Upgrading the Air Intake
To upgrade the air intake, start by removing the old air intake and installing a high-performance air intake system. Then, use a wrench to tighten the air intake's mounting bolts to the specified torque (20 Nm).
#### Step 3.2: Upgrading the Exhaust System
To upgrade the exhaust system, start by removing the old exhaust system and installing a high-performance exhaust system. Then, use a wrench to tighten the exhaust system's mounting bolts to the specified torque (30 Nm).
#### Step 3.3: Upgrading the Engine Computer
To upgrade the engine computer, start by installing a high-performance engine computer tune. Then, use a laptop to upload the new tune to the engine computer.

## Pro Tips & Common Mistakes
