## Technical Deep Dive
The electric Porsche Boxster's system works by using a combination of electric motors, batteries, and power electronics to propel the vehicle. The dual-motor setup provides exceptional acceleration and handling capabilities. However, it also introduces new challenges, such as thermal management and battery durability. The battery management system (BMS) plays a critical role in maintaining the health and performance of the batteries.

Common failure points in the electric Porsche Boxster include the electric motors, batteries, and power electronics. The electric motors can fail due to overheating, while the batteries can degrade over time due to deep discharging, high temperatures, or improper charging habits. The power electronics can fail due to overheating, overvoltage, or overcurrent conditions.
