## Step-by-Step Guide
### Step 1: Oil Change
The oil change is a crucial maintenance task for the Ram 1500 Rumble Bee. Regular oil changes help to prevent engine damage and ensure optimal performance.
#### Step 1.1: Locate the Oil Drain Plug
The oil drain plug is located at the bottom of the engine, near the front of the truck. Use a 10mm socket to remove the plug and allow the oil to drain into a pan.
#### Step 1.2: Replace the Oil Filter
Use an oil filter wrench to remove the old oil filter and discard it. Install a new oil filter and hand tighten it.
#### Step 1.3: Refill the Engine Oil
Use a funnel to pour in the new engine oil. Check the owner's manual for the recommended oil type and capacity.

### Step 2: Filter Replacement
The air filter and fuel filter should be replaced regularly to ensure optimal engine performance.
#### Step 2.1: Locate the Air Filter
The air filter is located in the engine compartment, on the driver's side. Use a screwdriver to remove the filter housing and pull out the old filter.
#### Step 2.2: Install the New Air Filter
Install the new air filter and replace the filter housing. Use a screwdriver to secure the housing.

### Step 3: Supercharger Maintenance
The supercharger is a critical component in the Hellcat-powered Ram 1500 Rumble Bee. Regular maintenance is essential to prevent failure.
#### Step 3.1: Check the Supercharger Oil
Check the supercharger oil level and top it off as needed. Use a torque wrench to tighten the oil cap.
#### Step 3.2: Inspect the Supercharger Belt
Inspect the supercharger belt for signs of wear or damage. Replace the belt if necessary.

## Pro Tips & Common Mistakes
* Always use the recommended oil type and capacity to prevent engine damage.
* Never over-tighten the oil drain plug or filter housing, as this can cause damage to the engine or filter.
* Use a torque wrench to ensure proper tightening of bolts and screws.
* Don't neglect regular maintenance, as this can lead to significant engine damage and costly repairs.
