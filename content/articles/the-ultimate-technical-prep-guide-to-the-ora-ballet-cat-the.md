## Step-by-Step Guide
### Introduction to Maintenance
The Ora Ballet Cat's maintenance is relatively straightforward, but it requires attention to detail to ensure longevity and performance.

#### Checking the Engine Oil
1. Locate the oil dipstick under the hood.
2. Pull out the dipstick and wipe it clean with a rag.
3. Insert the dipstick back into the oil reservoir and pull it out again.
4. Check the oil level against the minimum and maximum marks on the dipstick.

#### Replacing the Air Filter
1. Locate the air filter housing in the engine compartment.
2. Open the housing by releasing the clips or screws.
3. Pull out the old air filter and discard it.
4. Install a new air filter, ensuring it is properly seated and the housing is closed securely.

### Advanced Maintenance
#### Fuel Injector Cleaning
1. Purchase a fuel injector cleaning kit.
2. Follow the kit's instructions to connect it to the fuel system.
3. Run the cleaning process as per the kit's guidelines.

#### Spark Plug Replacement
1. Locate the spark plugs in the engine.
2. Remove the spark plug wire or coil pack.
3. Use a spark plug socket to remove the spark plug.
4. Install a new spark plug, ensuring it is properly gapped.
5. Replace the spark plug wire or coil pack.
