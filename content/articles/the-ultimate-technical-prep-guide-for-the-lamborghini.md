## Step-by-Step Guide
### Preparation
The first step in preparing your Fenomeno Roadster for optimal performance is to ensure the engine is properly warmed up. This involves driving the vehicle for at least 10 minutes to allow the engine to reach its optimal operating temperature.

#### Checking the Oil Level
Next, check the oil level to ensure it is at the recommended level. The oil level should be checked using the dipstick, and the level should be between the minimum and maximum marks.

#### Replacing the Oil Filter
The oil filter should be replaced every 5,000 miles to ensure the engine is properly lubricated. To replace the oil filter, locate the oil filter housing and remove the filter using an oil filter wrench.

#### Replacing the Spark Plugs
The spark plugs should be replaced every 30,000 miles to ensure proper engine performance. To replace the spark plugs, locate the spark plug wires and remove the spark plug using a spark plug socket.

### Turbocharger Maintenance
The turbochargers should be maintained every 10,000 miles to ensure proper performance and prevent overheating. To maintain the turbochargers, locate the turbocharger housing and remove the turbocharger using a turbocharger maintenance kit.

#### Cleaning the Fuel Injectors
The fuel injectors should be cleaned every 15,000 miles to ensure proper engine performance. To clean the fuel injectors, locate the fuel injector housing and remove the fuel injectors using a fuel injector cleaning kit.

#### Inspecting the Aerodynamics
The aerodynamics of the Fenomeno Roadster should be inspected every 10,000 miles to ensure proper downforce and handling. To inspect the aerodynamics, locate the rear spoiler and diffuser and inspect for any damage or wear.

## Pro Tips & Common Mistakes
* Always use the recommended type of fuel for the Fenomeno Roadster, as using the wrong type of fuel can lead to engine knock and premature wear.
* Never over-rev the engine, as this can lead to engine damage and premature wear.
* Always warm up the engine properly before driving, as this can help prevent engine damage and premature wear.
