## Sales Comparison
The Toyota Tacoma is on track to have its best sales year in a decade, outpacing its competitors. In contrast, the Jeep Wrangler has consistently been the best-selling off-road vehicle in its class.

### Key Statistics
The following table highlights key statistics for the Jeep Wrangler and its competitors, including the Toyota Tacoma:
| Vehicle | Sales (2025) | Off-Road Capability | Price Range |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Jeep Wrangler | 200,000+ | High | $28,000 - $43,000 |
| Toyota Tacoma | 250,000+ | Medium | $26,000 - $38,000 |
| Ford Bronco | 150,000+ | High | $30,000 - $45,000 |
| Toyota 4Runner | 120,000+ | Medium | $36,000 - $50,000 |

## Impact on Competitors
The success of the Jeep Wrangler and Toyota Tacoma has put pressure on their competitors to improve their off-road capabilities and sales strategies. The Ford Bronco and Toyota 4Runner have been working to catch up, but still lag behind in terms of sales.
