## Step-by-Step Guide
### Upgrading Your Jeep's Seats
#### Removing the Old Seats
1. Start by removing the seat bolts using a 10mm socket.
2. Gently pull the seat away from the floor and disconnect the electrical connectors.
3. Remove the old seat and set it aside.
#### Installing the New Seats
1. Place the new seat in position and reconnect the electrical connectors.
2. Tighten the seat bolts using a torque wrench (30 ft-lbs).
3. Adjust the seat to your desired position and test the functionality.
### Upgrading Your Jeep's Dashboard Trim
#### Removing the Old Trim
1. Start by removing the trim screws using a drill press.
2. Gently pull the trim away from the dashboard and disconnect the electrical connectors.
3. Remove the old trim and set it aside.
#### Installing the New Trim
1. Place the new trim in position and reconnect the electrical connectors.
2. Tighten the trim screws using a drill press (10 ft-lbs).
3. Adjust the trim to your desired position and test the functionality.
### Upgrading Your Jeep's Electrical System
#### Upgrading the Wiring Harness
1. Start by disconnecting the battery and ensuring the vehicle is secure.
2. Remove the old wiring harness and set it aside.
3. Install the new wiring harness, ensuring all connections are secure and tightened (10 ft-lbs).
4. Reconnect the battery and test the electrical system.
