## Step-by-Step Guide
In this section, we'll provide a comprehensive step-by-step guide to preparing your garage for the 2026 Ford F-150 XLT.

### Setting Up Your Garage
#### Step 1: Clearing the Workspace
Clear a space in your garage that is large enough to accommodate the 2026 Ford F-150 XLT. Remove any obstacles, including tools, equipment, and storage containers.

#### Step 2: Installing a Lift
Consider installing a lift in your garage to make it easier to work on the vehicle. A lift will give you better access to the underside of the vehicle and reduce the risk of injury.

#### Step 3: Organizing Your Tools
Organize your tools and equipment in a logical and accessible manner. Consider investing in a tool chest or storage cabinet to keep your tools tidy and within reach.

### Maintaining Your Vehicle
#### Step 1: Checking the Oil
Check the oil level in your 2026 Ford F-150 XLT regularly. Use a dipstick to check the oil level and top it up as necessary.

#### Step 2: Rotating the Tires
Rotate the tires on your 2026 Ford F-150 XLT every 5,000 to 8,000 miles. Use a tire rotation pattern to ensure even wear and extend the life of the tires.

#### Step 3: Inspecting the Brakes
Inspect the brakes on your 2026 Ford F-150 XLT regularly. Check the brake pads for wear and replace them as necessary.
