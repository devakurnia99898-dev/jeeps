## Technical Deep Dive
The new Hyundai Elantra features a range of advanced technologies, including a 2.0L inline-4 cylinder engine, an 8-speed automatic transmission, and a suite of safety features such as forward collision avoidance and lane departure warning. The vehicle's suspension system has also been redesigned, featuring a MacPherson strut front suspension and a torsion beam rear suspension.

Common failure points in the new Elantra include the turbocharger, which can be prone to failure due to excessive heat and wear. The vehicle's advanced safety features also rely on a complex network of sensors and cameras, which can be susceptible to damage or malfunction.
