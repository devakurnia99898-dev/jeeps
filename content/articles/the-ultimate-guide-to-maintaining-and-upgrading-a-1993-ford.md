## Step-by-Step Guide
### Oil Change
The oil change is one of the most important maintenance tasks for your 1993 Ford Thunderbird Super Coupe. It's recommended to change the oil every 5,000 to 7,500 miles.
#### Draining the Oil
1. Locate the oil drain plug, which is usually located at the bottom of the engine.
2. Use a 10mm socket to remove the plug and drain the oil into a pan.
3. Replace the plug and tighten it to the specified torque.

#### Refilling the Oil
1. Use a funnel to pour in the new oil.
2. Check the owner's manual for the recommended oil type and capacity.
3. Replace the oil cap and start the engine to circulate the new oil.

### Tune-up
A tune-up is essential to keep your engine running smoothly and efficiently.
#### Spark Plug Replacement
1. Locate the spark plugs, which are usually located on the side of the engine.
2. Use a spark plug socket to remove the spark plugs.
3. Gap the new spark plugs to the specified gap.
4. Install the new spark plugs and tighten them to the specified torque.

#### Fuel Injector Cleaning
1. Use a fuel injector cleaning kit to clean the fuel injectors.
2. Follow the instructions provided with the kit.
3. Use a fuel pressure test kit to check the fuel pressure.

### Brake Pad Replacement
The brake pads should be replaced every 30,000 to 50,000 miles.
#### Removing the Wheels
1. Loosen the lug nuts using a lug wrench.
2. Raise the vehicle using a jack and remove the wheels.

#### Removing the Brake Pads
1. Use a screwdriver to remove the caliper bolts.
2. Use a wrench to remove the caliper.
3. Remove the old brake pads and discard them.

#### Installing the New Brake Pads
1. Install the new brake pads and tighten the caliper bolts.
2. Tighten the lug nuts to the specified torque.

## Pro Tips & Common Mistakes

* Use a torque wrench to ensure the correct torque is applied.
