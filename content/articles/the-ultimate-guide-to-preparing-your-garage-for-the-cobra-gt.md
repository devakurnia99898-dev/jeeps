## Technical Deep Dive
The Cobra GT Coupe's V8 engine is a marvel of modern engineering, producing 720 horsepower and 640 lb-ft of torque. The engine features a dual-overhead camshaft design, with four valves per cylinder and a compression ratio of 9.5:1. The engine is paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission, which provides lightning-quick shifts and optimal power delivery. The car's suspension system features a double-wishbone design at the front and a multi-link setup at the rear, with adaptive dampers and anti-roll bars to provide exceptional handling and stability. Common failure points on the Cobra GT Coupe include the engine's timing chain, the transmission's clutch packs, and the suspension's bushings and ball joints.
