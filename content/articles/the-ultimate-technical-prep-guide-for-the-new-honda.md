### Preparing for Maintenance
To prepare for the new Ridgeline, it's essential to understand its maintenance requirements. This includes regular oil changes, tire rotations, and brake pad replacements.

#### Oil Change
1. Locate the oil drain plug and position a drain pan underneath.
2. Remove the oil drain plug and allow the oil to drain.
3. Replace the oil filter and refill the engine with new oil.
4. Tighten the oil drain plug to the specified torque (30 ft-lbs).

#### Tire Rotation
1. Locate the tire rotation pattern in the owner's manual.
2. Loosen the lug nuts on the first wheel using a torque wrench.
3. Raise the vehicle using a jack and remove the lug nuts.
4. Rotate the tire to the next position and hand tighten the lug nuts.
5. Lower the vehicle and tighten the lug nuts to the specified torque (80 ft-lbs).

#### Brake Pad Replacement
1. Locate the brake caliper and remove the caliper bolts.
2. Slide the caliper off the rotor and remove the old brake pads.
3. Install the new brake pads and slide the caliper back over the rotor.
4. Tighten the caliper bolts to the specified torque (25 ft-lbs).
