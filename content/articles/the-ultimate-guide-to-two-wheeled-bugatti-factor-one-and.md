## Step-by-Step Guide
### Step 1: Inspect the Braking System
The braking system is a critical component of the Bugatti Factor One. To inspect the braking system, follow these steps:
#### Step 1.1: Remove the Wheel
Remove the wheel to access the braking system. Use a 10mm socket to loosen the wheel nuts, then raise the vehicle using a hydraulic lift or jack stands.
#### Step 1.2: Inspect the Brake Pads
Inspect the brake pads for wear and tear. Check the thickness of the brake pads and look for any signs of damage or excessive wear.
#### Step 1.3: Check the Brake Fluid
Check the brake fluid level and top it off as needed. Use a brake fluid test strip to check the condition of the brake fluid.

### Step 2: Adjust the Suspension
The suspension system is critical to the handling and stability of the Bugatti Factor One. To adjust the suspension, follow these steps:
#### Step 2.1: Locate the Suspension Adjustment Points
Locate the suspension adjustment points, which are typically located near the shock absorbers.
#### Step 2.2: Adjust the Suspension
Use a suspension adjustment tool to adjust the suspension to the recommended settings. Consult the owner's manual for specific instructions.

### Step 3: Inspect the Electrical Components
The electrical components of the Bugatti Factor One require regular maintenance to ensure optimal performance. To inspect the electrical components, follow these steps:
#### Step 3.1: Check the Battery
Check the battery for signs of wear or damage. Clean the terminals and check the battery water level (if applicable).
#### Step 3.2: Inspect the Wiring
Inspect the wiring for signs of damage or wear. Check for any loose connections or corrosion.
