## Technical Deep Dive
The Rivian R2's electric powertrain is at the heart of its performance capabilities. Electric vehicles like the R2 utilize one or more electric motors, powered by a battery pack that stores electrical energy. The battery management system (BMS) is critical for ensuring the longevity and health of the battery pack by controlling charging and discharging rates, voltage, and temperature.

Common failure points or areas for improvement in electric vehicles can include the battery pack's capacity to hold charge over time, the efficiency and power output of the electric motors, and the thermal management system's ability to keep the batteries and motors within optimal operating temperatures. For a performance-oriented version, enhancements to these systems, such as more efficient motors, advanced battery chemistries, or improved cooling systems, could be key areas of focus.
