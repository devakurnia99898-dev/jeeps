## Technical Deep Dive
The ID. Cross features a 77 kWh lithium-ion battery pack, which is comprised of 12 modules, each containing 24 cells. The battery pack is managed by a sophisticated battery management system (BMS), which ensures optimal performance, range, and longevity. The electric motor is a permanent magnet synchronous motor, which provides excellent efficiency and performance.

Common failure points in electric vehicles like the ID. Cross include the battery pack, electric motor, and power electronics. The battery pack is susceptible to degradation over time, which can affect its range and overall performance. The electric motor can also be prone to overheating, which can reduce its efficiency and lifespan.
